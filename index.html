FAQs

How do I order?

First, you need to sign up for an account. After that, you can start add your favorite book to cart. Then, go to the cart, check if everything you need is there. Finally, click "Check Out" button to choose payment method and enter address.

Do you provide worldwide shipping?

Unfortunately, at the moment, we do not have this option.

What is your return policy?

You can return the book if the book is still sealed. In case the book is damaged in shipping process, you need to provide evidence so we can support your request.

Do you have physical store?

We are still working on our physical store, please stay tune!
